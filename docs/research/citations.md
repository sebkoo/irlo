# Research citations

Verified primary sources for statistics used in this repo. Rule: a stat may be
cited in README/docs only if it appears here with figure, population, year,
primary URL, and the date we accessed it.

## C-01 — WHO: 1 in 6 people worldwide affected by loneliness

- **Figure:** 1 in 6 people worldwide affected by loneliness; linked to an
  estimated ~100 deaths every hour (≈871,000 deaths annually, 2014–2019 basis).
- **Population:** global, all ages; loneliness most common among adolescents and
  young adults (~1 in 5) and in lower-income countries (~1 in 4).
- **Source:** WHO Commission on Social Connection, flagship report *From
  loneliness to social connection — charting a path to healthier societies*,
  published 2025-06-30.
- **URL:** <https://www.who.int/news/item/30-06-2025-social-connection-linked-to-improved-heath-and-reduced-risk-of-early-death>
  (report hub: <https://www.who.int/groups/commission-on-social-connection/report>)
- **Accessed:** 2026-07-10 (via web search verification in-session)
- **Used in:** `README.md` → "Why Irlo"

## C-02 — Harvard MCC: 21% of U.S. adults report loneliness

- **Figure:** 21% of U.S. adults feel lonely; 34% of young adults report
  loneliness; 73% of respondents name technology as a driver of loneliness in
  America.
- **Population:** nationally representative survey of U.S. adults, fielded
  May 2024.
- **Source:** Harvard Graduate School of Education, Making Caring Common project,
  *Loneliness in America: Just the Tip of the Iceberg?* (2024).
- **URL:** <https://mcc.gse.harvard.edu/reports/loneliness-in-america-2024>
- **Accessed:** 2026-07-10 (via web search verification in-session)
- **Used in:** `README.md` → "Why Irlo"
